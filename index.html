Word “CREDITO” Means

The word “Credito” is a combination of two different words Credit and Over from where the single word is derived “Credito” from Credit +O.

The word “Credito” means to come over to one’s credit (debts) or to help himself/herself which is also the main concept of the company. In another word the company helps people to save money. Therefore, we are here to help people who suffer from heavy bills of Medicals, clinics, lab, spa, beauty parlour, garment stores, accessoriesetc. by providing discounts in his/her bills of Medicals in our tied-up centers.
