Possibility

As per our healthcare business structure of India, every healthcare house (Hospital /Diagnostics…) spend minimum of 20% to 40% for their business development and this expense must bear our patients community for that we are going to make a wide patients community via a low-cost Health Card. Our Health Card members will get every discount from our affiliated healthcare houses with the specific discount which is discussed during the tie-up meeting and generally, this discount will not be available in market except the affiliated centers. If the Hospitals, Nursing homes, Diagnostics, Medicine, Spa, Beauty Parlors, Restaurant, Garment Shops, Shoes Show Room, Electronic Accessories, tec., will get patients or clients through Credito without any single marketing expenses or any other investment like Dr.Commission then the Hospitals, Nursing homes, Diagnostics, Medicine, Spa, Beauty Parlors, Restaurant, Garment Shops, Shoes Show Room, Electronic Accessories tec., authorityshould honor our Health Card holders and give them discount as per the discount is fixed during the meeting. Patients or the clients will go to Hospitals, Nursing homes, Diagnostics, Medicine directly with Credito Health Care Card.
